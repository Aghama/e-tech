Work With Us

To implement trainings and provide career mentorship for beneficaries of our programs, we engage the magnanimity of mission driven professionals who are experts in their various fields of technology to provide instruction and coaching for the mums and women who signup to join #DWBC as a way to empower themselves and/or grow their career and increase earnings.

Over 24 volunteer facilitators have worked with us so far, delivering lectures and mentoring women in the fields of Data, Programming, Product Design, Cloud Computing, Digital Storytelling & Design.

We seek to raise more awareness, encourage the entry and retention of more women in tech careers by providing tech skills training to women in Nigeria and other Anglophone African countries.

We continue to welcome trainers and mentors to be part of our mission. We are currently recruiting volunteer trainers for Cohort 5.
